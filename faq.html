Product

Once the picture is converted, the resolution will be lower than the original. For such reason, close-up pictures will provide the best results, especially for small formats.

Lighting is also very important. For best results, choose an evenly illuminated picture with a good exposure.

If you wish to have more than one subject in the final picture, please consider a bigger format (e.g. 80cm x 80cm) and make sure that the subjects are close together.

A high resolution image is not srtrictly needed, as long as the provided image has good lighting and exposure and the subject is clearly distinguishable.

In case of close-ups (faces only), as a rule of thumb, each subject will need a 40cm x 40cm space in the final mosaic picture.
